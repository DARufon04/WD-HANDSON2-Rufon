Naruto Uzumaki is the main character of Naruto, a spirited and determined ninja who seeks to gain recognition from his village and achieve his dream of becoming Hokage, the village leader.

Despite having a powerful demon fox sealed inside him, Naruto is driven by a strong sense of justice and a desire to protect his friends and loved ones.
