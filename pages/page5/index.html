Monkey D. Luffy is the main protagonist of One Piece.He's an adventurous and cheerful pirate with the ability to stretch his body like rubber due to eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy is on a quest to find the legendary treasure, the One Piece, and become the Pirate King. His unwavering determination and loyalty to his friends drive him through countless challenges and adventures.
